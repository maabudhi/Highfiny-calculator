What is an EMI Calculator?

An EMI Calculator is a financial tool used to estimate the fixed monthly payment, known as the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI), that a borrower needs to pay to repay a loan over a specific period. It helps users understand how much they will need to pay each month based on the total loan amount, the interest rate, and the loan tenure. To calculate EMI, three main components are required: the loan amount (principal), which is the total money borrowed; the interest rate, which is usually annual but converted to a monthly rate for calculation; and the loan tenure, which is the duration of the loan in months or years.

How to Use This EMI Calculator

  1. Enter your loan amount (₹5,000 to ₹10 crore)
  2. Select duration type (Years or Months)
  3. Enter your loan duration (1 to 40 years or 2 to 11 months)
  4. Input your annual interest rate (1% to 40%)
  5. View your monthly EMI and total repayment details instantly below

Benefits of EMI Calculator

  • Accurate Projections: Get precise estimates of your monthly payments based on your loan parameters
  • Financial Planning: Helps in budgeting by showing your monthly obligations before taking a loan
  • Quick Comparisons: Easily compare different loan scenarios by adjusting amount, tenure and interest rates
  • Time-Saving: Eliminates the need for manual calculations, providing instant results
  • Informed Decisions: Understand the total interest payable and make better borrowing decisions

What mathematical approach does this EMI Calculator use?

This EMI calculator uses the standard EMI calculation formula with monthly compounding. The formula used is: EMI = [P × r × (1 + r)^n] / [(1 + r)^n - 1], where P is the loan amount (principal), r is the monthly interest rate (annual rate divided by 12), and n is the loan tenure in months. This formula provides the fixed monthly payment that includes both principal and interest components, ensuring the loan is paid off in full by the end of the tenure.
