What is a SIP Calculator?

A "systematic investment plan" or "SIP" calculator is an online financial calculating tool that helps estimate the potential returns earned from regularly investing in mutual funds. Instead of focusing on how the investment grows, it specifically calculates how much profit or return you can expect to earn based on your monthly investment amount, investment period, and the expected rate of return. This helps investors understand how much they might gain from their total invested amount over time.

How to Use This SIP Calculator

  1. Enter your monthly SIP amount (₹100 to ₹10 crore)
  2. Enter your investment duration (1 to 50 years)
  3. Input your expected annual return rate (1% to 35%)
  4. Optionally enable and set an annual SIP increment (0% to 50%)
  5. View your estimated returns instantly just below

Benefits of SIP Calculator

  • Accurate Projections: Get precise estimates of your mutual fund returns based on your parameters
  • Financial Planning: Helps in planning your monthly investments by showing potential returns
  • Goal Setting: Understand how much you need to invest monthly to reach your financial goals
  • Increment Analysis: See how annual increments in your SIP can boost your returns

How SIP Returns are Calculated?

This SIP calculator uses the compound interest formula with monthly compounding. The formula used is: FV = P × [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r] × (1 + r), where FV is the future value of the investment, P is the monthly investment amount, r is the monthly rate of return (annual rate divided by 12), and n is the number of months. For investments with annual increments, each year's SIP amount is increased by the specified percentage before applying the formula.
